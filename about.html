    The name of this calander came from "Spoon Theory". This theory states that each person only has a limited amount of energy each day that they can spend on any given task. This energy is measured in spoons as a way of visualizing how much energy an activity takes.

    The goal of this calander is to help people to better visualize where their energy, or spoons, are being spent and to assist in better managing their energy so that they can avoid burnout and focus on whatever is most important to them.
